General Faq:
  • In order to play the game you must first create an account and sign in. When you create an account you will automatically be directed to the sign in page and once you have successfully signed in you will be redirected to the game page.
  • Your score will be displayed in the middle of the screen above the game. If you get an answer correct you will be awarded the $ amount represented for the question. If you get the answer incorrect you will be deducted the $ amount.
  • To start playing the game, click on the $ amount for the category and $ amount you wish to go for. When you click on the $ amount you will be redirected to the question. If you answer the question correctly you will be awarded the $ amount for this question, otherwise if you get it wrong you will be deducted the $ amount.
  • Your answers are not case sensitive and you are able to have whitespaces in your answers.
  • You will have 30 seconds to answer the question before you will be redirected to the main game page.
  • If you fail to answer the question within the alloted time you will still be penalized and the $ amount will be deducted.
  • When a question has been visited you will not be able to visit that question again and the question will display a strike through the $ amount indicating that you can't access the question anymore.
  • Once all questions have been visited you will be displayed a "Game Over" message and you will have the option to restart.
  • To restart the game you must hit play again.
